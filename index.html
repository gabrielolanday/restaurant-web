Hey, everybody! Welcome to Scott's Tots! In 2000, I promised a class of adorable underprivileged third graders that if they excelled academically, then I would pay their college tuition through the Michael Scott Foundation. The media and I affectionately called them "Scott's Tots". 9 years later I had to crush their hopes and dreams by telling them that I actually couldn't afford to pay for their tuition. I had no idea college even cost that much money! But because of this, I decided I had to give back to my community, and also somehow get rid of the crippling guilt of ruining the lives of a dozen young adults. So I opened Scott's Tots!

Here at Scott's Tots, each tater tot is made with the most love and care imaginable. Just like our slogan says, we want you to be afraid of how much you love our tots!

- Michael Scott, Founder of Scott's Tots, Regional Manager Dunder Mifflin Scranton
